Our Mission

We are here to stuff your craving! Whether that craving is for something sweet and delicious or for supporting local farmers we will surely satisfy it! We source as many ingredients as we can from Montana farmers by using the Western Montana Growers COOP along with serving Wilcoxson’s Ice cream . With our variety of toppings there is truly something for everyone. If we can stuff 406 into it you know we will!

Location and Hours

We are located in the heart of Missoula on 221 W Broadway! We are open Monday-Wednesday 8pm-11pm, Thursday 7am-1pm & 8pm-1am, Friday-Saturday 7am-1pm & 8pm-3am, and Sunday 7am-12pm!

How it Works

First, you start out with a fresh homemade donut! Then you choose your ice cream flavor or cream cheese frosting! Next, choose one topping and one sauce. Then, it is all pressed together and you can enjoy your masterpiece! We also have a variety of other treats such as our ice cream cookie sandwiches, our ice cream sundaes, and our milkshakes!
